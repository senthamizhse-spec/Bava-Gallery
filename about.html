About Bava

Bavadharini “Bava” Baskaran is a visual artist whose work explores color, texture, and movement. She began creating art at a young age, steadily developing her practice with encouragement from her family. Today, Bava shares her pieces through this gallery and finds balance and joy in swimming, which informs the rhythm and flow present in her artwork.
